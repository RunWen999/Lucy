About Lucy.

A Japanese Streetwear Brand

"City Boys Love to Have Fun in Da City"

Lucy. is a Japanese streetwear brand, founded by Imaishi Hiroyuki in 2019, located at Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan. Lucy. is heavily inspired by the American streetware and military. Lucy. are focusing on the city boys who are care about their lifestyle. The core concept of Lucy. is let city boys have fun in the city.
